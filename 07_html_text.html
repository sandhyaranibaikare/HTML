Environmental Protection

Environment is the most important part of human life. It provides us air, water, food, and shelter. Today, pollution and deforestation are creating many problems for nature. Therefore, it is our duty to protect the environment.

Importance of Trees

Trees give us oxygen and maintain ecological balance. We should never cut trees unnecessarily and instead plant more trees. A single tree can help many living organisms survive.

Pollution Control

Pollution affects human health badly. Water pollution, air pollution, and noise pollution are increasing day by day. We should use public transport and reduce plastic usage.

Save Water

Water is precious. Only 3% of Earth's water is fresh water. We should save water for future generations.

Scientific Facts

Water formula is H2O and plants release O2.

Einstein's famous equation is E = mc2.


In conclusion, everyone should take responsibility for protecting nature. A clean and green environment leads to a healthy and happy life.
